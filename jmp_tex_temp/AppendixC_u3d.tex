\section*{Appendix C. Interactive 3D Uptake Surface}

This appendix provides an interactive 3D visualization of the predicted vaccine uptake
surface derived from the multinomial logit model. The surface illustrates how the
probability of acceptance varies jointly with vaccine delay (0--6 months) and monetary
incentives (0--800 RMB).
