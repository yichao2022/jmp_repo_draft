# Social-Economic Analysis of AI Governance in Health Care Systems

## Topic Description
This project examines the governance of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies within health care systems, focusing on the interaction between technological innovation, regulatory frameworks, and socio-economic impacts. The research will explore how AI integration affects accessibility, equity, and efficiency in health care delivery, particularly in publicly funded systems.

## Why This Topic Interests Me
AI’s rapid advancement promises transformative potential for health care, yet raises complex governance challenges. Balancing innovation with ethical, social, and economic concerns is critical. Understanding this interplay aligns with my interest in technology policy and economic regulation, and its implications for public welfare.

## Relevant Theories
*   Political Economy of Technology and Innovation
*   Governance and Regulation Theory
*   Health Economics and Public Policy
*   Socio-technical Systems Theory

## Tentative Research Questions
1.  How do different regulatory approaches shape the adoption and impact of AI in health care?
2.  What are the socio-economic consequences of AI-driven health care reforms on vulnerable populations?
3.  How can policies balance innovation incentives with equity and ethical considerations?
